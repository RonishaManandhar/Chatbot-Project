Ticket Workflow
How to Handle New Tickets
  • Open the New Tickets page to view unassigned customer requests.
  • Click Join / Claim to take responsibility for a ticket.
  • Once claimed, the ticket moves into your assigned work queue.
  • Open the chat and respond to the customer professionally.
  • Update the ticket status as the issue progresses.
Live Chat
How to Use Live Chat
  • Click Enter Chat or Open Chat from the dashboard.
  • Customer messages appear live without refreshing.
  • Attachments uploaded by customers appear in both chat and attachment section.
  • Use clear, short, helpful responses.
  • Use the FAQ Library to copy accurate approved answers.
Escalation
When to Escalate to Admin
  • Escalate if the issue is outside your permission level.
  • Escalate if the customer reports a serious system problem.
  • Escalate if the customer is abusive or the issue is sensitive.
  • Escalate if payment, account deletion, or admin-only action is required.
Status Guide
Ticket Status Meanings
  • Open: Ticket has been created but not fully handled yet.
  • Pending: Agent is working on it or waiting for customer response.
  • Solved: Issue appears resolved but may still need confirmation.
  • Closed: Ticket is finished and chat becomes read-only.
  • Escalated: Ticket has been passed to admin for higher-level support.
Priority Guide
Priority Levels
  • Low: General question or non-urgent request.
  • Medium: Normal support issue needing attention.
  • High: Important issue affecting customer use.
  • Urgent: Serious issue that should be handled immediately.
