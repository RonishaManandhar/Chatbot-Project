Tone Settings
Chatbot Tone

Choose how the chatbot should sound when replying to customers.

Recommended for customer support: Professional or Simple.
Response Length
AI Answer Length

Control whether chatbot answers should be short, balanced, or detailed.

Short is useful for quick answers. Detailed is useful for step-by-step support.
Confidence Settings
AI Confidence Threshold

Set the minimum confidence level before the chatbot gives an AI answer.

Recommended: 70. Lower values allow more AI answers. Higher values make escalation more likely.
Escalation Settings
Human Support Escalation

Control when chatbot conversations should be escalated to a human support agent.

Auto Escalation Enabled If enabled, messages containing escalation keywords will suggest human support.
Separate keywords with commas. Example: refund, angry, complaint, urgent, manager, lawyer

Add custom instructions that guide how the chatbot should respond to customers.
